Corinthia Hotel

A sensitive refurbishment of signature suites and the creation of meeting rooms and function terraces within this elegant and palatial landmark hotel, one of the finest 5-star hotels in the city. The new treatment is a contemporary mix of striking colours with Art Deco geometries, bespoke furniture, art and luxury accessories.

The highlight of the new meeting rooms is the stunning Royal Lounge within the atrium, where clusters of super-sized lights create intimacy and screening from upper floors.

    Corinthia Hotels
  • Location:
    Budapest
  • Completion:

  • Scope
